Library Policies


For issuing:
  • You can issued the book if the available number of copies of the book for issue is greater than zero.
  • You can only hold one issued copy of the book at any given time.
  • The books will be available in the Issued Books section.
  • Normal members will have to return the book within 7 days of issuing, or the access to the book will be automatically revoked. They will be able to issue at most 5 books at a time.
  • Subscribed members will have to return the book within 14 days of issuing, or the access to the book will be automatically revoked. They will be able to issue at most 10 books at a time.
  • Subscribed members will also get the access to text-to-speech version of the book within those period.
For purchasing:
  • Before purchasing, you have to fill up the payment details section of the account page.
  • You can purchase the book anytime you want, regardless of the available number of copies for issue status of the book.
  • You purchase only one copy of the book.
  • The purchased books will be available for download in the Purchased Books section.
For become a subscribed member:
  • You have to fill up the payment details section of the account page.
  • Then register via the membership section of the account page.
  • The return date of the books issued before becoming a member will remain the same. So you will enjoy the benefits of extended return dates only on the books that you issue after becoming a member.
